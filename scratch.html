The 8 Main Islands

    1. Big Island
      • The official island:
        • Color: Red
        • Flower: Lehua
      • The biggest island in the state of Hawai'i



    2. Maui
      • The official island:
        • Color: Pink
        • Flower: Lokelani
      • The second biggest island in the state of Hawai'i



    3. Kaho'olawe
      • The official island:
        • Color: Gray
        • Flower: Hinahina
      • Is uninhabitable as it was used for military practices



    4. Lana'i
      • The official island:
        • Color: Orange
        • Flower: Kaunaoa
      • Onced produced 75% of the world's pineapples



    5. Moloka'i
      • The official island:
        • Color: Green
        • Flower: Kukui Nut



    6. O'ahu
      • The official island:
        • Color: Yellow
        • Flower: Ilima
      • The state capitol, Honolulu, is located here



    7. Kauai
      • The official island:
        • Color: Purple
        • Flower: Mokihana
      • Popular for The Na Pali Coast (a mountain range that stretches over 17 miles)



    8. Ni'ihau
      • The official island:
        • Color: White
        • Flower: White Pupu
      • Visiters are only allowed by invitation as it is owned by the Robert family
